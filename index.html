Who is Chandresh Sharma ?

I’m Chandresh Sharma — a BCA student, frontend developer, and someone who genuinely enjoys building clean and interactive digital experiences. I like turning simple ideas into modern websites that not only look good but also feel smooth and easy to use.

Most of my work revolves around HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, and UI/UX design. From creating the DemuRup Media website to building Pocket Bhajan Diary, I enjoy working on projects where design and functionality come together naturally.

I’m constantly learning, experimenting with new ideas, improving my frontend skills, and exploring ways to create better user experiences through modern web design.
